The Fujitsu Axess キーボードMCR (キーボード is Japanese for 'Keyboard') is a 108-key Japanese layout POS (Point of Sale) keyboard. It has 8 relegendable keys (F1 through to F8) and an integrated card reader in the top-right corner of the keyboard. Many of the keys appear to have more than one function accessible via either 'Ctrl' or 'Ctrl+Shift', although this is untested.

The MCR employs Topre capacitive switches and thin-walled keycaps. The fact that is has thin-walled keycaps is interesting as Topre generally make thicker keycaps. Despite the thinner, lighter keycaps, Topre is likely the OEM (Original Equipment Manufacturer). The MCR also has height adjustable feet and interfaces via PS/2 connector.
