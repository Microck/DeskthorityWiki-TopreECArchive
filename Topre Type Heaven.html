Type Heaven (typeset TYPE HEAVEN) is a keyboard brand produced by Topre since 2013. It is intended as a low-cost alternative to the popular Realforce line of keyboards. Topre have been making capacitive switch keyboards since 1983 for various companies in fields such as banking, broadcasting and data entry.

History

The Type Heaven was introduced at Computex 2013.[2] From the start, Topre authorized distribution and technical support for North America has been handled by Seasonic USA. Seasonic USA, a subsidiary of Taiwanese Sea Sonic Electronics Co., Ltd., has also extended Topre original 1 year warranty to 2 years, dramatically increasing Type Heaven's value. North American keyboard retailer Elite Keyboards was the first to start selling the Type Heaven on Amazon.com in July, 2013.

In January 2014, South Korean retailer 'Leopold' introduced a localised version of the keyboard with Hangul legends.[3].

In June, 2015, Topre were present at Computex where they demonstrated a new version of the Type Heaven with backlit RGB legends.[4] Reportedly, the backlit model has Cherry MX compatible sliders and an adjustable actuation point with settings for 1mm, 1.5mm, 2mm, 3mm.[4] They also teased additional Type Heaven models with coloured cases, although these models appear to have the standard laser-etched keycaps. The backlit model with adjustable actuation points will be priced slightly higher than the standard Type Heaven and is expected to be released in 2016.[4] It is possible that the Type Heaven RGB was simply a proof of concept that may never see production, or a stand-in as Topre prepared a new case for the Realforce RGB keyboard, slated for a US release in November, 2016.

Design details

General construction

The Type Heaven uses uniformly weighted 45 gram Topre switches and has the same metal-plate mounting as Realforce keyboards. Its case styling is new and appears to have a smaller footprint than Realforce keyboards. In particular, the Type Heaven's back edge has been reduced and rounded over, while its sides and front edge have sharper lines. Underneath, there are height-adjustable feet and a cable routing channel allowing the user to run the cable out from the left or right edge of the keyboard.

Keycaps

With the exceptions of the spacebar on the Realforce and re-legendable keycaps on some Topre OEM keyboards, the Type Heaven is the first Topre keyboard to have ABS keycaps rather than PBT. The keycaps are interchangeable with standard ANSI Realforce 104 keyboards. The legend printing method is laser etching. The gold colour of the legends is achieved as a direct result of the laser etching process and a coating is applied over the top to improve legend durability. Details of the keycaps used on the RGB backlit version of the Type Heaven are not yet known as Topre did not allow Computex 2015 attendees to remove keycaps.

Outsourced components

One or more of the Type Heaven's components are made in China,[2] presumably to reduce its price in comparison to the Japan-made Realforce keyboards. Topre have confirmed that at least one component, the switch springs, are Chinese-made. Other parts, such as the rubber dome sheet and likely the sliders and their housings, are made in Japan by Topre. The rubber dome sheets use the same material and construction process as those in Realforce keyboards.
