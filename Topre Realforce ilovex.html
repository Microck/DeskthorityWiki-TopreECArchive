The Topre Realforce ilovex keyboard is a limited edition (300 pieces) 108-key Japanese layout keyboard made by Topre for ilovex to celebrate ilovex's 20th anniversary. The 'REALFORCE' logo normally present on the LED indicator panel is replaced by 'ilovex System development', but this keyboard is essentially the same as Realforce 108U-A (XE0100) in layout. The ilovex keyboard has uniformly weighted 45 cN keys, a bright green enclosure, and bright orange keycaps. It also comes with green replacement keys for 'I', 'L', 'O', 'V', 'E', 'X', '2' and '0', once again in recognition of ilovex's 20th anniversary.

The ilovex keyboards were intended as gifts for ilovex employees and business partners, however, they can be purchased on Amazon Japan for ¥29,800 (~€280 or $365 USD) and have been previously sold with a personalised letter and ilovex branded calendar, pictured below.
