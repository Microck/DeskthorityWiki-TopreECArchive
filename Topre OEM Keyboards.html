Introduction

In addition to the Realforce brand of keyboards, Topre are also the OEM of a number of keyboards used in various environments including accounting/banking, aviation, broadcasting, medical and the gaming/entertainment industries.

Criteria

This list consists of keyboards that meet one or more of the following criteria:

  • Are prototype boards that were not officially released or mass produced.
  • Do not have 'Realforce' or 'Type Heaven' labelling (e.g. Topre Short-throw, Topre JDL).
  • Have had 'Realforce' or 'Type Heaven' labelling removed or partially removed (e.g. CS NEO OEM).
  • Use 'Realforce' or 'Type Heaven' labelling in addition to other prominent company branding (e.g. ilovex KEYBOARD, JUST Limited, DHARMA TACTICAL KEYBOARD).

Results may vary between keyboards, but in general, manufacturing dates will appear printed on stickers which are located on the PCB and (if present) mounting plate.

See the 'Manufacturing dates' section of the Realforce page for more information.
