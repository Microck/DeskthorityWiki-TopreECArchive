Introduction

The μTRON is an ergonomic keyboard with Topre switches by UC Technology and Personal Media Corporation. Originally designed by Dr. Ken Sakamura, the μTRON is also known or referred to as "MTRON" and "microTRON".

Specifications

Number of Keys 76
Supported Layouts JIS (QWERTY), TRON (DVORAK)
Printed Layout JIS (QWERTY)
Slope/Angle Front: 6° or 10° / Sides: 10°
Keycap Shape Cylindrical
Keycap Profile Sculpted / Stepped
Switch Topre Capacitive
Key Pitch 17 mm
Keystroke 4 mm
Key Weight Uniform 45 g
Dimensions W: 161.5 mm (per side) × D: 141.2mm × H: 42.5 mm (legs folded)
Net Weight 720 g
Interface USB (Mini-B female). Supplied with 1.8 m USB-A male cable
Other Firmware updatable
